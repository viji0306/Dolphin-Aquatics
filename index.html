Welcome to Dolphin Aquatics

Dolphin Aquatics, led by Dronacharya Awardee Mr. Nihar Ameen, is a premier swimming academy in India. Since 1984, we have been nurturing swimming talent, from beginners to Olympic champions. We are an AUSTSWIM Recognized Centre, ensuring high teaching standards and consistency. Our pathway begins from teaching beginners to learn to swim to coaching champions for the Olympics. There is no better place to start your swim journey, than here!
